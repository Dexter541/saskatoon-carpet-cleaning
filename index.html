About Us

At Saskatoon Carpet Cleaning, we use our carpet cleaning expertise combined with advanced technology to bring your carpet back to life, no matter what condition it is in. We offer steam cleaning (pressurized hot water extraction) and shampooing services, depending on the condition and the state of your carpet. Just know that you will be impressed with the results! Thankfully, 97% of our clients are repeat clients who have helped us build our reputation and grow our business by referring us to their friends and family. If you need our assistance and would like to hear about our work and reputation, ask us for a list of references who will testify their positive experience with us.

Our Staff

We ensure that our staff have the ability and the knowledge needed to bring your carpet to the level of cleanliness you desire. Since our service is defined by our staff’s performance, we ensure all the team members of Saskatoon Carpet Cleaning are Friendly, professional, and reliable. That they are Devoted to customer care and have training in the industry’s leading practices
