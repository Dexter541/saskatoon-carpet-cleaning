Our Services

Carpet Binding

carpet binding is a procedure that involves covering the edges of a carpet with a strip of fabric and then securely stitching it in place. Binding protects the edge of the carpet from wear and also prevents unraveling. The binding material can be made of various materials, such as polyester or cotton. The color can be anything; it can match so as to be virtually unnoticeable, or it be a contrasting colour which complements the carpet. Binding is typically the easiest and least expensive method of finishing a carpet edge.

Carpet Serging

Carpet Serging is a thick fiber, such as yarn, that looks like a continuous wrap-around stitch around the entire edge of a carpet. Like binding, serging can either be of a matching or contrasting colour, with the most common being matching. A serged edge is most commonly seen on manufactured area rugs.

Carpet Fringing

Fringing is a common and somewhat iconic finishing treatment for area rugs. This is a look that is often seen: long fringed tassels, usually a white or off-white color. In hand-knotted rugs, the fringe is necessary, as it is the edge of the “backbone” fibers of the area rug. On machine-made rugs, however, fringe is only for appearance and serves no practical purpose..

Area Rug Cleaning

At Advance Carpet Services, we provide you with the best experience, and set the standard for outstanding quality. We have the expertise and the equipment required to ensure professional cleaning. Our wide range of cleaning services, offer a variety of solutions that can tackle even the toughest of stains, while expertly preserving the quality of your carpets. In order to clean your area rugs,

  • We use advanced machinery to vibrate the rug, which loosens any sand and debris that are trapped in the yarn
  • We vacuum both side of the rug, to remove all debris; making sure we have a clean surface to work on
  • We shampoo and rinse the rug, (paying special attention to stained areas), using variety of machines depending on the type of rug and the type of colors used on the rug. If the rug is delicate, we take extra care of the rug. For such rugs, we hand shampoo a small portion of the rug at a time, then we extract the soap and water within seconds of washing the area to eliminate any possibility of damaging the rug.
  • We hang the rug to dry. This allows both side of the rug to dry properly.
  • We check on the rug half-way through the drying process to make sure the carpet has cleaned properly, and the stains have been removed (to the best of our ability). If the rug needs further cleaning, we repeat the cleaning process.
  • When we are satisfied with the cleaning, we make sure the rug is fully dried
  • We vacuum the rug again, prior to delivering it to our wonderful clients
